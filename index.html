Our Mission

We, the parish family of St. Mark o Jamii in Busia, pledge to be a welcoming community united in the Eucharist. We strive to live as disciples of Christ as we worship, teach, and serve together in the spirit of 'Harambee'. By the grace of the Holy Spirit, we journey together towards eternal life.

Small Christian Communities

Our parish is organized into vibrant Small Christian Communities (Jumuiya Ndogo Ndogo). These neighborhood-based groups are the heartbeat of our parish, where we meet for prayer, Bible sharing, and mutual support.

Faith Sharing

Weekly meetings to reflect on the Sunday Word and share how it applies to our daily lives in Busia.

Mutual Support

Supporting one another during joyful times and challenges, ensuring no one in our community walks alone.

Parish Service

Each Jumuiya takes turns leading liturgical celebrations and participating in parish maintenance projects.

Harambee Spirit

Working together in the spirit of Harambee to support our parish development and outreach programs.
